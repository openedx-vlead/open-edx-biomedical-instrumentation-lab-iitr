1) To study the wave shapes of ECG signals under incorrect electrode placements recorded by Lead I, Lead II (reference lead RL) and Chest leads (V1-V6) ECG system.
2)To study the wave shapes of ECG signal for 120 different abnormalities for example-premature contraction, arrhythmias, ventricle focus, different BPM, fibrillations, different rhythms , with noise, cardiac abnormalities etc.
3) To review various features of ECG signal and wave components such as ST interval, QT interval and R-R interval.
4) To assess your knowledge about the experiment by answering a questionnaire at the end of the experiment.
